SCARY GAME

Context

You've just moved in but you're still not feeling at home...

You have noticed some very strange things happening in the house
that makes you believe that someone else is living with you...

BUT WHO ?

You went to the police but they laughed at you and said:

"- Madam, you better not do drugs or drink so much when you're alone"

I DO NOT DO DRUGS! I do crossfit .-.

I'm determined to get evidence to throw in these cops' faces. For that,
I'm going to get my camera and I'm going to photograph this
person!

I just hope that person doesn't get upset with me, I just want to be
able to live alone in my house. If she has nowhere else to go, I can
even help find her a new location, but I just need to know WHO that
person is.

Instructions

The game will start after pressing the "start" button.Your objective is
to capture the ghost inside the camera, which will be represented by this
image below. To do this, you must click on the "take picture"
button when the object is exactly inside the camera's square.
To play again, you must press the "play again" button and then "start"


Attention: If the object is not completely within the selected area, you will
not win the game.

You will come across other obstacles that will make it difficult to capture the
ghost on camera. Furthermore, time is also your enemy. That way, you
only have 30 seconds maximum to take the picture.

Good luck!

WARNING: If you are sensitive to terrifying sounds or macabre images, this game
should not be played.
